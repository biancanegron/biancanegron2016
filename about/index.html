About Bianca Negron

Bianca Negron is a designer & developer living in NYC. She became fascinated with the World Wide Web ever since her mom purchased Apple's Bondi Blue iMac computer. Later on, she graduated with a degree in New Media Technology. She currently works with Universal Music Group as a Senior Digital Designer. She is passionate about creating and building meaningful experiences. When Bianca is not glued to her computer she can be found playing guitar, enjoying some good whiskey with friends, traveling, taking pictures of places/people/things, or attempting to play catch with her cat, Jade.
